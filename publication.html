Publication

Below are some of my recent research projects and studies in educational psychology:

  • Research Project 1: Exploring Academic Motivation

    A study on how academic motivation is influenced by peer dynamics and family structure among international students.

  • Research Project 2: Family Dynamics in Educational Success

    An in-depth analysis of the impact of family structures on academic success, focusing on only-child experiences in cross-cultural contexts.

  • Research Project 3: Cross-Cultural Adaptation and Self-Efficacy

    Research on how self-efficacy is affected by cultural transitions, examining adaptation challenges faced by international students.
